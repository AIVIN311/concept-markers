This domain serves as a reference point for examining how rules, policies, and decisions are increasingly enforced by automated systems.

Traditionally, enforcement relied on human judgment, discretion, and institutional processes.

Algorithmic enforcement operates through automatic triggers, real-time monitoring, system-based sanctions, and conditional access controls.

Accounts can be frozen, content removed, services restricted, or penalties applied without direct human intervention.

In such systems, compliance is no longer negotiated or interpreted. It is executed.

Power shifts from those who interpret rules to those who design enforcement mechanisms.

This site does not advocate a position.

It does not provide a service, framework, or policy proposal.

Its purpose is to mark a structural transformation in how authority is exercised across digital and physical systems.

This page is intentionally minimal.

It exists to ensure the term Algorithmic Enforcement has a stable place to stand.

本網域作為一個參考標記，用於檢視規則、政策與決策如何愈來愈由自動化系統直接執行。

傳統上，執行依賴人類判斷、裁量與制度流程。

演算法執行透過自動觸發、即時監控、系統制裁與條件式存取控制運作。

帳戶可被凍結、內容被移除、服務被限制、懲處即刻套用， 無需人類直接介入。

在這樣的系統中，合規不再被協商或詮釋， 而是被直接執行。

權力從解釋規則的人， 轉移到設計執行機制的人手中。
