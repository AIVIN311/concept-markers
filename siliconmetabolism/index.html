This domain serves as a reference point for examining how digital and AI infrastructures consume energy, resources, and environmental inputs in patterns resembling biological metabolic systems.

Silicon metabolism does not refer to individual devices or data centers alone.

It emerges through global computing networks, large-scale AI training, cloud infrastructures, cooling systems, and resource extraction.

In such systems, technological growth becomes tied to continuous material consumption and environmental impact.

This site does not promote sustainability strategies or energy solutions. It does not provide efficiency metrics.

Its purpose is to mark a systemic process already unfolding across technological development and planetary resource use.

This page is intentionally minimal.

It exists to ensure the term Silicon Metabolism has a stable place to stand.
