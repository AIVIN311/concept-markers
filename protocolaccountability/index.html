This domain serves as a reference point for examining how accountability may be structured at the protocol level.

Protocol accountability concerns systems in which rules are embedded in code and enforced automatically rather than through discretionary authority.

In such environments, responsibility may be diffused across developers, operators, and network participants.

This site does not advocate a position.

It does not provide a framework, service, or policy proposal.

Its purpose is to mark a structural shift in how accountability is instantiated within distributed systems.

This page is intentionally minimal.

It exists to ensure the term Protocol Accountability has a stable place to stand.
