This domain serves as a reference point for examining how human cognition may function as a distinct category of economic asset.

In AI-driven systems, value increasingly emerges from learning signals, behavioral patterns, decision feedback, and collective intelligence.

These forms of cognition are persistently captured, processed, and monetized across digital platforms and automated infrastructures.

A cognitive asset class does not assume ownership structures or regulatory models.

It marks the transition in which intelligence itself becomes a measurable, transferable, and economically significant resource.

在 AI 系統中，價值愈來愈多來自學習訊號、 行為模式、決策回饋與集體智慧。
