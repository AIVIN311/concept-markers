## Body Copy

### EN

- Hybrid Personhood names the continuity problem that appears when identity must persist across both biological and synthetic substrates.
- It is not only a question of whether an entity is "human" or "artificial," but whether recognition can remain coherent when agency is distributed across both.
- The concept becomes visible in shared systems where memory, decision support, delegated action, and representation are split between people and computational agents.
- In these environments, personhood status and identity continuity do not fully overlap: one concerns recognition, while the other concerns persistence across transitions.
- Hybrid personhood therefore marks an operational threshold where institutions must decide what counts as the same actor over time, context, and substrate.
- At system scale, hybrid personhood requires sovereignty decisions over compute access, identity registries, and protocol control when human and synthetic agents are jointly represented.
- This site does not argue for legal recognition rules, institutional reforms, or technical identity standards.
- It does not provide certification, compliance, or identity verification services.
- Its purpose is to mark a structural identity problem emerging across governance systems, digital infrastructures, and human-machine coordination spaces.
- This page deliberately maintains minimalism so the term can remain stable while the surrounding institutions continue to shift.

### ZH

- 混合人格指認一個連續性問題：當身份必須跨越生物與合成基質維持時，如何仍被視為同一主體。
- 它不只是「人類」或「人工」的分類問題，而是當能動性分布在兩者之間時，承認是否仍能保持一致。
- 當記憶、決策支援、委任行動與外部表述被分散在人與計算代理之間時，這個概念會在共享系統中變得可見。
- 在這類環境裡，人格地位與身份連續性並不完全重合：前者關於承認，後者關於跨轉換的持續性。
- 因此，混合人格標記的是一個運作門檻：制度必須決定在時間、情境與基質改變之後，何者仍算同一行動者。
- 在系統尺度上，混合人格要求對算力存取、身份登錄與協定控制做出主權層級的決策，特別是在人體與合成代理被共同表述時。
- 本站不主張法律承認規則、制度改革或技術身份標準。
- 亦不提供認證、合規或身份驗證服務。
- 它的目的，是標示一個正在治理系統、數位基礎設施與人機協作空間中浮現的結構性身份問題。
- 本頁刻意維持極簡，使這個詞在周邊制度持續變動時仍能保持穩定。
