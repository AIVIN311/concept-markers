This domain serves as a reference point for examining how gold re-enters reserve design as a protocol layer when trust in purely fiat anchors weakens.

An aurum reserve protocol is not a return to a single metal standard.

It describes the rules, constraints, and operational procedures by which gold becomes usable as reserve backing: custody, assay, collateralization, settlement, and conversion pathways.

In such systems, the question is not whether gold “has value,” but how value is made legible, movable, and enforceable under stress.

This site does not advocate a monetary regime.

Its purpose is to mark the layer where physical reserves become programmable through standards, access control, and institutional interoperability.

「黃金儲備協議」並不等同於回到單一金本位。

它描述的是：讓黃金能被當成儲備支撐所需的一整套規則與操作程序—— 包含保管、鑑定、抵押化、交割結算、以及可兌換與流動性的通道設計。

在這種系統裡，核心問題不是「黃金是否有價值」， 而是價值如何在壓力情境下被標準化、可移動化、可執行化。

本站不主張任何貨幣制度。

它的目的，是標記一個層：實體儲備如何透過標準、存取控制與制度互通，變成可運作的協議結構。
