This domain serves as a reference point for examining how allocation authority is operationalized when distribution is systematized.

In this condition, control is exercised through quotas, queue design, priority tiers, and access gates.

Power is less about ownership and more about release conditions: rate limits, schedulers, cooldown windows, allowlists, denylists, and service refusal.

Allocation rules often appear not as policy language, but as system parameters and control plane defaults.

This site does not advocate a position.

It does not provide a service, framework, or policy proposal.

Its purpose is to name a governance condition in which allocation operates as infrastructure.

This page is intentionally minimal.

It exists to ensure the term Algorithmic Allocation Systems has a stable place to stand.

本網域作為一個參考標記，用於檢視：當分配被系統化後， 控制如何透過配額、佇列、優先級與存取閘門被執行。

在此狀態中，權力不是「擁有」，而是「可否放行」：速率限制、排程器、冷卻時間、黑白名單、服務拒絕。
