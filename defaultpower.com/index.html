This domain serves as a reference point for examining how power is exercised through the ability to set defaults.

Default power does not require coercion. It operates by defining what happens when no explicit choice is made.

In many systems, the most consequential decision is not which option is chosen, but who defines the baseline state, the opt-out path, and the cost of deviation.

This site does not argue for or against choice architecture.

Its purpose is to name a form of authority that often appears as convenience, standardization, or neutral design — while producing real governance effects.

This page is intentionally minimal.

It exists to ensure the term Default Power has a stable place to stand.

本網域作為一個參考標記，用於檢視：權力如何透過「設定預設」而被行使。

預設權力不必依賴強迫。 它的運作方式是：在沒有明確選擇發生時，先決定「事情會怎麼走」。

在許多系統中，最關鍵的並非人們選了哪個選項， 而是誰能定義基準狀態、退出路徑，以及偏離預設所需承擔的成本。

本站不主張支持或反對選擇架構。

其目的是為一種常被包裝成便利、標準化或中立設計的權威命名—— 但它會產生真實的治理效果。

本頁刻意維持極簡。
