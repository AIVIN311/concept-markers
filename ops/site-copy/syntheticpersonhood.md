## Identity Sentence

**EN:** Synthetic Personhood formalizes the recognition of artificial agents as identity-bearing entities capable of participating in legal and social systems.

**ZH:** 合成人格將人工代理作為身份承載實體的承認加以形式化，使其得以參與法律與社會系統。

## Body Copy

### EN

- This domain serves as a reference point for examining the conditions under which synthetic entities may be treated as persons within governance systems.
- Synthetic personhood is not simply a philosophical debate about machine consciousness or moral status.
- It becomes operational when artificial agents perform actions that require attribution, responsibility, and legal or institutional recognition.
- When systems must determine whether a synthetic entity can hold rights, bear obligations, or be subject to sanctions, personhood becomes a threshold decision rather than a descriptive label.
- Under such conditions, recognition depends on governance criteria, accountability structures, and enforceable identity frameworks rather than on biological origin.
- Synthetic personhood separates functional capability from legal recognition, and legal recognition from moral standing.
- Power therefore depends not only on whether an artificial agent can act, but on who defines the criteria for recognizing that action as attributable to a person-like subject.
- This site does not advocate for granting or denying legal personhood to artificial entities.
- Its purpose is to name a governance condition in which synthetic agency intersects with responsibility, liability, and institutional control.
- This page deliberately maintains minimalism so the term Synthetic Personhood can retain a stable foothold as artificial agents continue to expand in capability and autonomy.

### ZH

- 本網域作為一個參考標記，用於檢視：在何種條件下，合成實體可能在治理系統中被視為具有人格。
- 合成人格並不只是關於機器意識或道德地位的哲學辯論。
- 當人工代理的行動需要被歸屬、承擔責任，並取得法律或制度承認時，這個問題就會變得具體。
- 當系統必須判斷合成實體是否能持有權利、承擔義務或接受制裁時，人格成為一個門檻決定，而非描述性標籤。
- 在這樣的條件下，承認取決於治理標準、責任架構與可執行的身份框架，而非生物來源。
- 合成人格將功能能力與法律承認區分開來，也將法律承認與道德地位區分開來。
- 因此，權力不只在於人工代理是否能行動，而在於誰制定那些使其行動被歸屬於「類人格主體」的標準。
- 本站不主張賦予或拒絕合成實體法律人格。
- 它的目的，是為一種治理條件命名：合成能動性正在與責任、賠償與制度控制交會。
- 本頁刻意維持極簡，使 合成人格（Synthetic Personhood） 這個詞在人工代理能力與自主性持續擴張時仍能保有穩定立足點。
