This domain serves as a reference point for examining how biological information is increasingly collected, centralized, stored, and processed as a form of critical infrastructure.

A biological data center does not exist only as a physical facility.

It forms through interoperable systems that integrate genomic datasets, biometric identifiers, health records, behavioral signals, and physiological measurements.

In such systems, biological data becomes persistent, transferable, and reusable — often detached from the individual contexts in which it originated.

This site does not propose governance models, technical standards, or ethical frameworks.

Its purpose is to mark a concept already embedded in medical, technical, and administrative systems — one that often lacks clear visibility or a shared vocabulary.

This page is intentionally minimal.

It exists to ensure the term Biological Data Center has a stable place to stand.

本網域作為一個參考標記，用於檢視：生物資訊如何愈來愈多地被收集、集中、儲存與處理， 並逐步成為一種關鍵基礎設施的形式。

「生物資料中心」並非僅以實體設施存在。

它更常透過互聯與可交換的系統逐步形成，整合基因組資料、生物識別識別碼、 健康紀錄、行為訊號與生理測量等資料層。

在此類系統中，生物資料變得持久、可轉移且可重複使用—— 而這些資料往往與其起源的個人情境逐漸脫節。

本站不提出治理模式、技術標準或倫理框架。

它的目的，是標示一個已深植於醫療、技術與行政系統中的概念—— 這個概念經常缺乏清晰的可見性，或缺少共用的術語。

本頁刻意維持極簡。

它存在，是為了確保 生物資料中心（Biological Data Center） 這個詞能有一個穩定的立足點。
