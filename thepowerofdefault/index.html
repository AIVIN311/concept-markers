The Power of Default

預設權力
預設權力 預設選項 默認效應 選擇架構 助推 現狀偏誤 系統預設 結構性影響

This domain serves as a reference point for examining how outcomes are shaped by preset conditions rather than explicit choice.

Defaults do not compel action. They operate by remaining in place when no action is taken.

In many systems, the default option becomes the dominant path, not because it is preferred, but because deviation requires effort, knowledge, or resistance.

This site does not argue against choice architecture or behavioral design.

Its purpose is to name a structural force that influences decisions without appearing as authority or command.

This page is intentionally minimal.

It exists to ensure the term The Power of Default has a stable place to stand.

本網域作為一個參考標記，用於檢視：結果如何經常由「預設條件」所形塑，而非由明確的選擇所決定。

預設並不強迫行動。它的力量來自於：當人們沒有採取行動時，它仍然維持原狀。

在許多系統中，預設選項會成為主要路徑， 並非因為它更受偏好，而是因為偏離預設需要額外的成本、知識，或抵抗。

本站不主張反對選擇架構或行為設計。

它的目的，是為一種結構性力量命名：它能影響決策， 卻不以權威或命令的形式現身。

本頁刻意維持極簡。

它存在，是為了確保 預設權力（The Power of Default） 這個詞有一個穩定的立足點。
