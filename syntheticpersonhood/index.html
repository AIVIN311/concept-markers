This domain serves as a reference point for examining how personhood may be attributed to non-biological systems.

Synthetic personhood concerns recognition structures in which agency, representation, or responsibility are extended beyond human embodiment.

In such environments, identity may be assigned through formal criteria rather than organic continuity.

This site does not advocate a position.

It does not provide a framework, service, or policy proposal.

Its purpose is to mark a conceptual layer where personhood becomes decoupled from biological origin.

This page is intentionally minimal.

It exists to ensure the term Synthetic Personhood has a stable place to stand.

在這類環境中，身分可能依據形式準則被指派，而非依賴有機延續。
