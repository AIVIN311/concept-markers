This domain serves as a reference point for examining how technological futures emerge unevenly across societies, regions, and systems rather than arriving simultaneously.

The future does not appear as a single moment of transformation. It manifests first in isolated environments—labs, corporations, military systems, privileged infrastructures, or regulatory exceptions.

Over time, fragments of that future diffuse outward, while large populations continue to operate within older technological conditions.

This site does not predict timelines or promote emerging technologies. It does not provide forecasts or innovation roadmaps.

Its purpose is to mark a structural pattern: progress arriving asymmetrically, shaping power, access, and opportunity.

This page is intentionally minimal.

It exists to ensure the term The Future Is Already Here (It Is Just Not Evenly Distributed) has a stable place to stand.

本網域作為一個參考標記，用於檢視：科技未來如何以不均勻的方式 出現在不同社會、區域與系統之中，而非同時降臨。

未來並非在某一刻全面轉變。 它往往先出現在孤立環境中——實驗室、企業系統、軍事架構、 特權基礎設施或特殊監管空間。

隨著時間推移，未來的片段逐漸向外擴散， 同時仍有大量人口運作在舊有技術條件之中。

本網站不預測時間表，也不推廣新興技術。 亦不提供趨勢預測或創新藍圖。

其目的是標示一種結構性模式： 進步以不對稱方式到來，並重塑權力、存取與機會。

本頁刻意保持極簡。

它存在，是為了確保 未來已然在此，只是分布不均 這個概念能有一個穩定的立足點。
