This domain serves as a reference point for examining the widening fracture between lived reality and artificial representations.

Synthetic reality crisis does not arise from a single technology such as deepfakes or virtual environments alone.

It develops through the convergence of generative media, simulation systems, automated narratives, and algorithmically constructed perception.

As synthetic content becomes embedded within everyday reality, trust and verification, shared understanding becomes increasingly unstable.

This site does not recommend technical defenses or policy interventions. It does not provide detection services or educational resources.

Its purpose is to mark a civilizational shift already unfolding across media systems, social platforms, and digital infrastructure.

This page is intentionally minimal.

It exists to ensure the term Synthetic Reality Crisis has a stable place to stand.
