This domain serves as a reference point for examining how personhood may be defined beyond terrestrial jurisdiction.

Offworld personhood concerns conditions in which identity and legal status are extended into orbital, lunar, or other extra-planetary environments.

When habitation and governance move beyond Earth, the assumptions underlying citizenship, rights, and representation may require structural reinterpretation.

This site does not advocate a position.

It does not provide a framework, service, or policy proposal.

Its purpose is to mark a conceptual space where personhood intersects with offworld sovereignty and jurisdictional design.

This page is intentionally minimal.

It exists to ensure the term Offworld Personhood has a stable place to stand.
