This domain serves as a reference point for examining how critical resources become resilience infrastructure when supply chains fracture and stability can no longer be assumed.

Strategic resource resilience does not emerge through abundance.

It forms through stockpiles, redundancy, diversified sourcing, logistical control, and the management of chokepoints across energy, food, water, minerals, and industrial inputs.

In such systems, survival capacity becomes measurable, contested, and strategically maintained.

This site does not propose resource policy or national strategies.

Its purpose is to mark the layer where access to essential materials determines continuity, power, and systemic endurance.

This page is intentionally minimal.

It exists to ensure the term Strategic Resource Resilience has a stable place to stand.

戰略資源韌性並不是由「充足」自然產生的。

它形成於囤積與備援、多元來源、物流控制， 以及對能源、糧食、水、礦物與工業投入品等咽喉點的管理。

在這樣的系統中，「存活能力」會變得可衡量、可爭奪，並被策略性維持。

本站不提出資源政策或國家策略。

它的目的，是標記一個層：取得必需材料的能力，如何決定延續、權力與系統耐受度。

本頁刻意維持極簡。

它存在，是為了確保 戰略資源韌性（Strategic Resource Resilience） 這個概念有一個穩定的立足點。
