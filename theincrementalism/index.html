This domain serves as a reference point for examining how large-scale change occurs through accumulation rather than declaration.

Incrementalism does not rely on disruption, rupture, or formal transition. It proceeds through small adjustments, pilot programs, provisional rules, and temporary measures that gradually become permanent.

At no single moment does the system appear to change, yet over time its direction, structure, and consequences are fundamentally altered.

This site does not criticize or endorse incremental approaches.

Its purpose is to mark a pattern that operates across policy, technology, and governance — often perceived as stability rather than transformation.

This page is intentionally minimal.

It exists to ensure the term The Incrementalism has a stable place to stand.

本網域作為一個參考標記，用於檢視：大型變化如何透過「累積」而非「宣告」發生。

「漸進主義」不依賴破壞、斷裂或正式轉場。 它透過小幅調整、試點計畫、暫行規則與臨時措施持續推進， 並在時間中逐步固化為常態。

在任何單一時刻，系統看起來都沒有明顯改變； 但長期累積之後，它的方向、結構與後果卻已被根本改寫。

本站不批判或背書漸進式方法。

它的目的，是為一種跨政策、科技與治理運作的模式命名—— 而這種模式往往被感受為「穩定」，而非「轉型」。

本頁刻意維持極簡。

它存在，是為了確保 漸進主義（The Incrementalism） 這個詞能有一個穩定的立足點。
