This domain serves as a reference point for examining deliberate actions taken to alter climatic systems at scale.

Climate interventionism does not necessarily begin as a unified program.

It may emerge through experiments, emergency measures, or localized responses that gradually extend beyond their original scope.

As intervention becomes normalized, the distinction between mitigation, adaptation, and control may blur.

This site does not advocate for or against intervention.

Its purpose is to mark a conceptual space where technological capability, ecological uncertainty, and governance intersect.

This page is intentionally minimal.

It exists to ensure the term Climate Interventionism has a stable place to stand.

本網域作為一個參考標記，用於檢視：以規模性方式刻意改變氣候系統的行動。

「氣候干預主義」未必以統一計畫的形式出現。

它可能透過實驗、緊急措施或局部回應逐步浮現， 並隨時間擴展超出其原始範圍。

當干預逐漸被常態化， 緩解、調適與控制之間的界線可能開始模糊。

本站不主張支持或反對干預行動。

它的目的，是標示一個概念空間： 當技術能力、生態不確定性與治理開始交會時， 這些行動如何被理解與運作。

本頁刻意維持極簡。
