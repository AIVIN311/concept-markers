This domain serves as a reference point for examining an emerging view of personal data: not as transferable goods, but as extensions of human existence.

Organic data rights do not arise only through privacy law or consent policies.

They emerge through biometric systems, behavioral tracking, digital identity frameworks, and continuous forms of data extraction directly connected to human life.

In such environments, data becomes inseparable from the person it represents.

This site does not propose legal reforms or advocacy campaigns. It does not provide compliance tools.

Its purpose is to mark a conceptual shift already forming across law, technology, and governance.

This page is intentionally minimal.

It exists to ensure the term Organic Data Rights has a stable place to stand.

本網域作為一個參考標記，用於檢視一種新興觀念：與其把個人數據視為可轉移的商品， 不如將其視為個體存在的延伸。

「有機數據權」並非僅因隱私法或同意政策而產生。

它透過生物識別系統、行為追蹤、數位身份框架， 以及與人類生命直接相關的持續資料擷取逐步浮現。

在這樣的環境中，數據與它所代表的人密不可分。

本站不提出法律改革或倡議活動， 亦不提供合規工具。

它的目的，是標示一個已在法律、科技與治理中逐步形成的概念轉變。

本頁刻意維持極簡。

它存在，是為了確保 有機數據權（Organic Data Rights） 這個詞能有一個穩定的立足點。
