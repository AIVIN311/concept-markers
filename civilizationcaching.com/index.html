This domain serves as a reference point for examining how civilizations store, accumulate, and reuse structures, knowledge, and systems over time.

Civilization caching does not occur only through archives or libraries.

It emerges through institutions, infrastructures, legal frameworks, technological platforms, cultural practices, and inherited systems that persist beyond their original context.

In such processes, past decisions, designs, and values remain embedded, shaping future possibilities and constraints.

This site does not propose models of preservation or reform.

Its purpose is to mark a structural phenomenon in which civilizations operate not only through innovation, but through accumulated layers of cached systems.

This page is intentionally minimal.

It exists to ensure the term Civilization Caching has a stable place to stand.

本網域作為檢視文明如何隨時間累積、保存與調用結構、知識與系統的參考點。

「文明緩存」並非僅存在於檔案館或圖書館之中。

它透過制度、基礎設施、法律架構、科技平台、 文化實踐與繼承系統逐步形成， 並長期存續於原始脈絡之外。

在這樣的過程中，過去的決策、設計與價值持續被嵌入， 形塑未來的可能性與限制。

本網站不提出保存策略或改革方案。

其目的是標示一種結構性現象： 文明的運作不僅依賴創新， 也依賴累積層疊的緩存系統。

本頁刻意保持簡約。
